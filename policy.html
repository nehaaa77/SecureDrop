Privacy Policy

At SecureDrop, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your data and ensure your privacy.

1. Data Collection

  • We do not collect any personal information
  • No user accounts or registration required
  • No tracking or analytics of user behavior
  • No cookies or local storage for tracking purposes

2. File Handling

  • All files are encrypted before storage
  • Files are automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • No file content is stored on our servers
  • Files are only accessible via unique share links

3. Security Measures

  • AES encryption for all file content
  • Blockchain verification for document integrity
  • Secure random key generation
  • Automatic cleanup of expired files
